Introduction

Crypto mining in 2025 is more competitive than ever, but starting a **small mining farm** is still a profitable venture if done correctly. This guide will help you plan your hardware, energy, and cooling systems to maximize ROI.

Step 1: Plan Your Budget and Goals

Before starting, determine:

  • Investment Size: How much can you spend on hardware and electricity?
  • Target Coins: BTC, LTC, DOGE, or profitable altcoins like KDA.
  • ROI Period: Aim for 8-12 months ROI with the right setup.

Step 3: Electricity and Power Setup

Electricity is your biggest expense. Tips:

  • Negotiate bulk power rates or industrial tariffs.
  • Use high-efficiency PSUs (80+ Platinum rated).
  • Consider **solar or hybrid energy** for long-term savings.

Step 4: Cooling Solutions

Overheating reduces miner efficiency and lifespan. Cooling options:

  • Immersion cooling for large setups.
  • Industrial fans with airflow optimization.
  • Dust filters for cleaner environments.

Step 5: Optimize with VNish Firmware

VNish firmware allows:

  • Advanced overclocking and underclocking.
  • Lower power consumption with tuning profiles.
  • Auto-switching between mining pools for better rewards.

Step 6: Select Mining Pools

For **Bitcoin**, pools like ViaBTC and F2Pool are popular. For **Litecoin/Dogecoin**, LitecoinPool offers merged mining and low fees.
